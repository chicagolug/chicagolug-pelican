GNU/Linux + Free/Libre & Open Source Software + Fun

Welcome! The ChicagoLUG is a community of GNU/Linux and Free software developers, enthusiasts and system administrators who live in and around Chicago, Illinois. We encourage participation from people of all age, gender, and ethnic groups, all sexual orientations, as well as all knowledge levels. If you're curious about Linux and Free and open-source software, consider joining us.
