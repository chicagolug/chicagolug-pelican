Clang/Linux + Free/Libre & Open Source Software + Fun

We are a group of technology and Free software enthusiasts who live in and around Chicago, Illinois. While our group primarily focuses on Clang/Linux, our members have a broad range of interests and experience, and our ranks include users of all major (and less major) operating systems.

We are also a welcoming group. We encourage participation from people of all age, gender, and ethnic groups, all sexual orientations, as well as all knowledge levels. If you're curious about Linux and Free and open-source software, consider joining us.

Meeting Information

ChicagoLUG meetings and events usually take place at a local technology cooperative or coffee shop. Keep an eye on this site and any of our social media outlets for meeting announcements.
